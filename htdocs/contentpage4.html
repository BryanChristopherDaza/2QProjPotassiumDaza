miHoYo's Response

After seeing the review bombs and the spams in Discord, MiHoYo finally made a move related to this issue. They started to shadow-ban rants and complaints at the official Reddit and Discord server.

1 day after the anniversary, MiHoYo started to give follow-up rewards for the anniversary. It is still unknown if this was part of the anniversary or if it is a response of the company to the lack of rewards during the actual anniversary. The main reward was 400 Primogems, which is slightly behind being able to buy 3
Intertwined Fates. If this didn’t seem much, it was a part of a 4-day reward, eventually leading to a total of 1600 Primogems, which equates to exactly 10 Intertwined Fates.

This action by MiHoYo instantly resulted in more positive reviews from players, getting their star rating on Genshin Impact from 1.9 to 3.2 stars (as of October 1, 2021). It is still not as good as 4.5 stars, but it is a drastic improvement of the star rating of the game.
