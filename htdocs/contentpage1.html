What is Genshin Impact?

Before we dive into the main topic, let me show you what Genshin Impact is. Genshin Impact is an action role-playing game developed and published by Chinese video game development and animation studio MiHoYo (Wikipedia, 2021). This game is a cross-platform game which you can play on mobile, both Android and Mac OS, PC, and PS4/PS5. The game was released on September 28, 2020, and has been a main product of the company since its first release, earning an astonishing 1 billion USD, 6 months after its release.



This game takes place in a continent known as Teyvat.
Teyvat is divided into seven nations; Mondstadt, Liyue, Inazuma, Sumeru, Natlan, Fontaine, and Snezhnaya, each corresponding to different elements. This game revolves around twins separated by an unknown god. The male twin must now find a way to find his sister, and takes on an adventure through the seven nations, asking the gods of the nations for information about her sister.

Since we are going to talk about the in-game rewards, let’s discuss the main thing players buy first: Intertwined Fates.

Intertwined fates are in-game objects the players of
the game use when they want to wish for a limited character. They are worth 160 Primogems, which are objects you can claim or receive from doing specific quests or events. Wishing is where a player uses 1 Intertwined Fate to see whether they get the limited character or not.
